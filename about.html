Our Journey

2019 - The Beginning

Our founders, inspired by their travels through Japan and deep appreciation for authentic ramen culture, began planning what would become Sakura Ramen with a simple vision: bring true Japanese ramen to Central Florida.

2020 - Perfecting the Craft

Months of recipe development, working with master chefs from Japan, and sourcing the finest ingredients. Every broth was tested hundreds of times until it met our exacting standards for authenticity and flavor.

2021 - Opening Day

After extensive preparation and training, Sakura Ramen opened its doors in Windermere, bringing authentic Japanese ramen to the local community. The response was overwhelming, confirming our belief in the demand for quality Japanese cuisine.

Today - Growing Community

We've become a beloved destination for ramen enthusiasts and newcomers alike. Our commitment remains unchanged: serve the finest and freshest foods while creating an authentic Japanese dining experience.
